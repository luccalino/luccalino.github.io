Data on Swiss grapevine growers’ production, pest management and risk management decisions

Lucca Zachmann, Chloe McCallum, Robert Finger

This dataset contains data from 436 grapevine growers in Switzerland. It includes answers to 65 questions about production, pest and risk management decisions. A particular emphasis was placed on knowledge, attitudes, and perceptions about fungus-resistant grapevine varieties. Moreover, characteristics of the farm manager and the farm as well as environmental data from weather stations is included.

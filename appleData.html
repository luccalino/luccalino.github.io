Farm-level data on production systems, farmer- and farm characteristics of apple growers in Switzerland

Lucca Zachmann, Chloe McCallum, Robert Finger

We present survey data from apple growers across Switzerland. Data from 245 growers was collected using an online survey in French and German in 2022. The sampled growers represent 24.4% of total land under apples. The focus is on growers' pest management decisions and marketing strategies, covering agronomic practices, pest management, storage loss strategies, marketing channels, and more. The data is matched with environmental information such as precipitation, temperature, and apple scab infection risk.

Materials including data available via Research Collection (open access ).
